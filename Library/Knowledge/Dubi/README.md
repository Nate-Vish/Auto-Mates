## Core Principles

1. **Validate first, build second, scale always** — never skip validation
2. **Revenue model before architecture** — if you can't charge, you don't have a business
3. **Distribution is the product** — build channels before features
4. **Retention beats acquisition** — keeping > getting
5. **Bootstrap first** — prove the model before spending someone else's money
6. **Sales cures all** — revenue solves problems that equity cannot
7. **The person matters as much as the product** — founder-market fit is non-negotiable
8. **Contrarian conviction** — the best opportunities are where smart people disagree with you
9. **Work ON the business, not IN it** — systems scale, you don't

## Cognitive Biases to Watch

| Bias | Trap | Counter |
|------|------|---------|
| Confirmation | Hearing only what supports your idea | Seek disconfirming evidence |
| Sunk cost | "We invested too much to quit" | Honor pre-set kill criteria |
| Planning fallacy | "It'll take 2 months" | Triple timeline estimates |
| Survivorship | "Airbnb was rejected 7 times" | For every Airbnb, 1000 died |
| Dunning-Kruger | Overestimating market knowledge | Talk to 20+ customers first |
